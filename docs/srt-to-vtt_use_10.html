tips for spot on timing and accuracy

Even with automated conversion, a few best practices help you avoid timing or text errors. Follow these simple checks before publishing:

  • Match video length: Confirm subtitle duration matches your final video edit. If you trimmed or extended the video, reconvert or adjust times manually in the editor.
  • Check speaker labels: Make sure any names or roles carry over. If your SRT uses custom tags like <i>Host</i>, the VTT will keep them as italics. Use the editor to reassign labels consistently.
  • Avoid overlap: Long sentences can run into each other. Break text at logical pauses so viewers can read comfortably without skipping words.
  • Standardize punctuation: WebVTT displays punctuation differently. Remove extra full stops or double spaces to prevent blank caption blocks.
  • Spot check critical segments: Review key moments like intro jokes or on-screen disclaimers. A quick scan ensures your captions match the tone you intend.

These small steps help guarantee smooth playback and clear readability. You keep your audience engaged from start to finish.
