Why convert SRT to VTT for your podcast captions?

SRT and VTT are both popular subtitle formats, but VTT offers a few key advantages for podcast video clips:

  • Accessibility compliance: Leading platforms and regulatory bodies prefer VTT for captions because it supports additional metadata and language tags.
