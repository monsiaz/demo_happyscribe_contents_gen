What is the main goal of converting SRT to VTT?

Many streaming platforms accept VTT so they can manage styling, text positioning, and other advanced features. The main goal of converting from SRT to VTT is ensuring your captions remain intact and in sync, while also meeting the technical demands of modern media players.
